What is Prakriti?

Prakriti in Ayurveda is an individual's unique constitution, determined by three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha (air, fire, and water). Understanding it guides personalized healthcare, disease susceptibility, diet, and treatment for holistic well-being.
