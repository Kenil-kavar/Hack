Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient system of natural healing that originated in India. It focuses on achieving and maintaining a balance of the body, mind, and spirit to promote overall health and well-being.

How Does Our Formulation Recommendation System Work?

Our system utilizes advanced data analysis and Ayurvedic principles to assess patient data. It then provides personalized recommendations for Ayurvedic formulations and remedies tailored to the patient's specific health condition.

How Can Patients Use Your System?

Patients can input data to discover their potential health conditions.

How Do Practitioners Get Formulations Based on Disease Name?

Practitioners simply provide the disease name, and our system generates the relevant formulations for them to use in treatment.

Can I Trust the Predictions Made by Your System?

Our system is designed based on Ayurvedic knowledge and modern data analysis techniques. While it can provide valuable insights, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare practitioner for accurate diagnoses and treatment.
